What Is Back Panel Advertising

Rear Vinyl — Direct & Affordable

Auto rickshaw back panel advertising places your brand on the rear of the rickshaw using durable printed vinyl. It's the surface every driver and pedestrian behind the rickshaw stares at while waiting in traffic — a captive, eye-level audience.

Back panel vinyl comes in multiple sizes: a 36×12 inch strip, a 36×24 inch medium, and a 36×36 inch full square. Smaller strips are budget-friendly for wide coverage, while the full square gives maximum impact and is visible from any angle.

Because the rear panel is read at close range and while stationary, it's ideal for QR codes, app-install prompts and short offers. Printed on weather-resistant vinyl and fitted by our team, with GPS-stamped proof of every installation across 200+ cities.
