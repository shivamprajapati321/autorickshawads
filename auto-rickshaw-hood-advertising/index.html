What Is Hood Advertising

Retro / Top Hood — Maximum Visibility

Auto rickshaw hood advertising (also called retro or top advertising) uses the curved canopy over the passenger cabin as a moving billboard. It covers the back, left, right and top — so your brand is visible from almost every angle on the road.

Because the hood sits high and faces oncoming traffic, it captures attention at exactly the moments people are watching the road: traffic signals, junctions, markets and railway stations. A single rickshaw travels around 150 km a day and is seen by roughly 10 people per km — that's about 1,500 daily impressions per vehicle.

Every hood is printed on premium rexine using solvent printing for weather-resistant, long-lasting colour, then stitched and fitted by our own team. With GPS-stamped installation photos, you can see exactly which rickshaws carry your brand and where they run — no blind spend.
